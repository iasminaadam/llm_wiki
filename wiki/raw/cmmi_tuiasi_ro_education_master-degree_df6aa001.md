**The Faculty of Machine Manufacturing and Industrial Management** from the “Gheorghe Asachi” Technical University of Iași offers seven specializations for Master’s Degree studies (2 years).

### Educational offerings of the master studies (2 years)

| Programme of Study/Specialization | DEPARTMENT COORDINATOR |
|---|---|
| Advanced Manufacturing Technologies (A.M.T.) | Machines Manufacturing Technology |
| Production Management and Technology (P.M.T.) | Machines Manufacturing Technology |
| Computer Aided Design and Manufacturing Systems (C.A.D.M.S.) | Machine Tools |
| Micromechanical Systems (M.M.S.) | Machine Tools |
| Applied Fluid Mechanics (A.F.M.) | Fluid Mechanics, Fluid Machinery and Fluid Power Systems |
| Engineering and Management in Mechanical Manufacturing (E.M.M.M.) | Machines Manufacturing Technology |
| Industrial Entrepreneurship (I.E.) | Machines Manufacturing Technology |
